How to Setup Ableton Live Sync (macOS)
  1. Open the built-in macOS Audio MIDI Setup application.
  2. Go to Window -> Show MIDI Studio -> double-click IAC Driver and ensure "Device is online" is checked.
  3. Open Ableton Live -> Preferences -> Link/Tempo/MIDI.
  4. Under MIDI Ports, find Out: IAC Driver (Bus 1) and toggle Sync to On.
  5. Refresh Pocket Lab, ensure the External Sync toggle above is active. When you click Play in Ableton, Pocket Lab will automatically lock tempo and begin grading your playing synchronously!
